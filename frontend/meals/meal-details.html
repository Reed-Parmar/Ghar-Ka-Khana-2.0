About This Meal

Our Paneer Butter Masala is a beloved North Indian classic that brings the authentic taste of home-cooked food right to your campus. Made with fresh paneer (cottage cheese) that's marinated and cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based gravy infused with aromatic spices, butter, and cream.

The dish is perfectly balanced with the right amount of spices and sweetness, making it a crowd favorite. Served with freshly made butter naans and fragrant jeera rice, this meal is complete comfort food that reminds you of home.

Perfect For:

  • Lunch or Dinner
  • Special occasions or treating yourself
  • Sharing with friends (can be split between 2 people)
